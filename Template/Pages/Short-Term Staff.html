We are looking for people who:

  • share the same values,
  • will commit to Greek language learning,
  • are flexible and adaptable,
  • are self motivated,
  • take initiative,
  • persevere,
  • can handle the different challenges that come with a pioneering situation,
  • don’t compromise easily.

Short‐Term commitment (3 months) requires:

  • involvement in team activities like worship, intercession, admin meetings, meals, fun times;
  • coming alongside existing ministries;
  • participating in community life;
  • familiarizing with the Greek culture;
  • the option of studying the language at Greek university.
