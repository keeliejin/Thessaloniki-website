We are looking for people who:

  • share the same values,
  • will commit to Greek language learning,
  • are flexible and adaptable,
  • are self motivated,
  • take initiative,
  • persevere,
  • can handle the different challenges that come with a pioneering situation,
  • don’t compromise easily.

Long‐Term commitment (2 years*) requires:

  • mandatory Greek language learning;
  • leading and being part of team activities like worship, intercession, admin meetings, meals, fun times;
  • pioneering and establishing a new ministry;
  • participating in community life.
